Asset Mail Matrix

One page showing every asset mail type plus the oversight copy lead, HCS, or MD would receive.

Routing Map Assigned Transfer Initiated Transfer Received Returned Status Alert Status Restored

Routing Map

Current implemented routing for oversight copies.

Assigned Person Type Main Recipient Oversight Copy Notes
Employee Employee Department Lead Lead copy is the immediate upward notification.
Department Lead Lead HCS and MD Any lead other than HCS and MD now routes upward to both HCS and MD.
HCS HCS MD MD gets the oversight copy.
MD MD None No further escalation copy.

There are 6 distinct user-facing asset mail variants in code. `system_restored` shares the same template as `asset_status_fixed`, so both are represented by “Status Restored”.
