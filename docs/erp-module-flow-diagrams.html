ACOB ERP Module Flow Diagrams

This document groups the 9 approved operational and performance-flow diagrams into one printable reference.

Modules covered: Leave Management, Help Desk, Project, Task, Reference Generator, Individual KPI, Individual PMS, My Work, and Goal.

1. Leave Management

Shows request creation, evidence checks, staged approvals, and final leave activation.

flowchart TD A["Employee opens Leave page"] --> B["Create leave request"] B --> C["Enter leave type, dates, reliever, handover details"] C --> D["System validates balance, overlap, reliever, and evidence rules"] D --> E{"Evidence required and missing?"} E -->|Yes| F["Request moves to pending_evidence"] E -->|No| G["Request moves to reliever approval"] F --> H["Employee uploads evidence"] H --> G G --> I{"Reliever decision"} I -->|Approve| J["Department lead approval"] I -->|Reject| X["Request rejected"] J --> K{"Lead decision"} K -->|Approve| L["HR / Admin approval"] K -->|Reject| X L --> M{"HR / Admin decision"} M -->|Approve| N["Leave approved"] M -->|Reject| X N --> O["Balance updated"] N --> P["Attendance / leave record effect applied"] N --> Q["Requester and approvers notified"]

2. Help Desk

Shows the support route, procurement route, ticket ownership, and KPI contribution paths.

flowchart TD A["Requester creates help desk ticket"] --> B{"Ticket type"} B -->|Support or service| C["Ticket enters department queue / assignment flow"] B -->|Procurement| D["Ticket moves to pending_approval"] C --> E{"Assigned?"} E -->|To department queue| F["Department manages queue workload"] E -->|To person| G["Assigned staff member owns execution"] F --> H["Ticket status moves through queue workflow"] G --> I["System syncs ticket to executable task"] I --> J["Synced task auto-links to assignee goal"] J --> K["Completed synced task contributes to individual KPI"] D --> L{"Procurement approval decision"} L -->|Approved| M["Approved for procurement"] L -->|Rejected| Z["Procurement request rejected / returned"] M --> N["Ticket enters execution flow"] N --> I H --> O["Department help desk performance contributes to department KPI"] G --> P["Status changes: assigned -> in_progress -> resolved -> closed"] P --> Q["Requester can comment and rate after resolution / closure"] P --> O

3. Project

Shows project setup, task creation, assignment modes, and how delivery contributes to KPI.

flowchart TD A["Admin or project manager creates project"] --> B["Members and ownership are defined"] B --> C["Project task is created"] C --> D{"Assignment type"} D -->|Individual| E["Assign to one project member"] D -->|Multiple| F["Assign to multiple project members"] D -->|Department| G["Assign to a department"] E --> H["Project task stored in tasks table"] F --> H G --> H H --> I{"Task has individual owner?"} I -->|Yes| J["Task auto-links to assignee goal"] I -->|No| K["Task remains department delivery work"] J --> L["Task completion contributes to individual KPI"] H --> M["Project delivery contributes to department KPI"] A --> N["Project updates and timeline are recorded"]

4. Task

Shows task assignment authority, task types, and how operational tasks move to measurable outcomes.

flowchart TD A["Task creation request"] --> B{"Who is assigning?"} B -->|Department lead| C["Lead has department-scoped authority"] B -->|HCS or MD| D["Global task assignment authority"] C --> E["Lead can assign to self"] C --> F["Lead can assign to staff in own department"] D --> G["HCS / MD can assign to anyone in any department"] E --> H["Task saved"] F --> H G --> H H --> I{"Assignment type"} I -->|Individual| J["Owned by one user"] I -->|Multiple| K["Owned by multiple users"] I -->|Department| L["Owned by department workflow"] J --> M["Task auto-links to owner goal"] K --> N["Task tracks shared completion"] L --> O["Task supports department delivery"] M --> P["Task status updates and completion contribute to KPI"] N --> P O --> Q["Department task delivery contributes to department KPI"]

5. Reference Generator

Shows correspondence creation, auto-filled sender details, and searchable record management.

flowchart TD A["User opens Reference Generator"] --> B["Sender details auto-fill from profile"] B --> C["User enters subject, recipient, status, and content details"] C --> D["Correspondence record is created"] D --> E["Reference number is stored"] E --> F["Record appears in correspondence table"] F --> G["Users search, filter, and paginate records"] G --> H["Reference can be found by number, subject, sender, or recipient"]

6. Individual KPI

Shows how approved goals receive evidence automatically from assigned work.

flowchart TD A["Approved employee goals exist"] --> B["Work is created for the employee"] B --> C["Manual task"] B --> D["Project task"] B --> E["Help desk synced task"] C --> F["System auto-links work to matching approved goal"] D --> F E --> F F --> G["Employee completes linked work"] G --> H["Goal score is calculated from linked task completion"] H --> I["Individual KPI = average of approved goal scores"]

7. Individual PMS

Shows the final employee performance score built from KPI, KSS, attendance, and behaviour.

flowchart TD A["Individual KPI score"] --> E["Individual PMS calculation"] B["KSS results score"] --> E C["Attendance score"] --> E D["Behaviour review score"] --> E E --> F["PMS = KPI x 70% + KSS x 10% + Attendance x 10% + Behaviour x 10%"] F --> G["Performance review is created or updated"] G --> H["Employee views and acknowledges review"]

8. My Work

Shows the unified personal work surface for executable assigned work.

flowchart TD A["User opens /work"] --> B["System loads assigned executable work"] B --> C["Manual tasks"] B --> D["Project tasks"] B --> E["Help desk synced tasks"] C --> F["Items merge into My Work list"] D --> F E --> F F --> G["User filters by search, status, source, and priority"] G --> H{"User opens item"} H -->|Task| I["Navigate to task management"] H -->|Project task| J["Navigate to project detail"] H -->|Help desk| K["Navigate to help desk ticket detail"]

9. Goal

Shows how a goal moves from target-setting into an active KPI bucket with linked work evidence.

flowchart TD A["Employee creates goal draft"] --> B["Lead or manager reviews and approves goal"] B --> C["Goal becomes active KPI target"] C --> D["System receives linked work evidence"] D --> E["Manual tasks"] D --> F["Project tasks"] D --> G["Help desk synced tasks"] E --> H["Goal progress updates from linked completion"] F --> H G --> H H --> I["Goal score contributes to Individual KPI"]
