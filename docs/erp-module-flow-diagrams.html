ACOB ERP Module Flow Diagrams

This version keeps each title and diagram together on the same landscape page and includes Department PMS as the 10th flow.

1. Leave Management

Request creation, evidence checks, staged approvals, and final leave activation.

flowchart TD A["Employee opens leave page"] --> B["Create leave request"] B --> C["Enter leave type, dates, reliever, reason, handover"] C --> D["System validates balance, overlap, reliever, and policy"] D --> E{"Evidence required?"} E -->|Yes| F["Request moves to pending evidence"] E -->|No| G["Reliever approval stage"] F --> H["Employee uploads evidence"] H --> G G --> I{"Reliever decision"} I -->|Approve| J["Department lead approval"] I -->|Reject| X["Request rejected"] J --> K{"Lead decision"} K -->|Approve| L["HR or Admin approval"] K -->|Reject| X L --> M{"HR or Admin decision"} M -->|Approve| N["Leave approved"] M -->|Reject| X N --> O["Balance updated"] N --> P["Attendance and leave effects applied"]

2. Help Desk

Support route, procurement route, ticket ownership, and KPI contribution paths.

flowchart TD A["Requester creates help desk ticket"] --> B{"Ticket route"} B -->|Support route| C["Department queue or direct assignment"] B -->|Procurement route| D["Pending approval"] C --> E{"Assigned to person?"} E -->|Yes| F["Execution owner works ticket"] E -->|No| G["Department queue handles workflow"] F --> H["Ticket syncs into task"] H --> I["Synced task auto-links to goal"] I --> J["Completion contributes to individual KPI"] G --> K["Queue progress contributes to department KPI"] D --> L["Requester department lead approval"] L --> M["Service department lead approval"] M --> N["Head Corporate Services approval"] N --> O["Managing Director approval"] O --> P["Approved for procurement"] P --> F F --> Q["Status flow assigned to in progress to resolved to closed"] Q --> R["Requester rates after resolved or closed"] Q --> S["Department help desk performance contributes to department KPI"]

3. Project

Project setup, task creation, assignment modes, and delivery contribution.

flowchart TD A["Admin or project manager creates project"] --> B["Project members and ownership are set"] B --> C["Project task is created"] C --> D{"Assignment type"} D -->|Individual| E["Assign to one member"] D -->|Multiple| F["Assign to multiple members"] D -->|Department| G["Assign to department"] E --> H["Task stored in tasks table"] F --> H G --> H H --> I{"Individual owner exists?"} I -->|Yes| J["Task auto-links to owner goal"] I -->|No| K["Task remains department delivery work"] J --> L["Task completion contributes to individual KPI"] H --> M["Project delivery contributes to department KPI"] H --> N["Project updates and notifications are recorded"]

4. Task

Assignment authority, task types, and how tasks move into measurable outcomes.

flowchart TD A["Task creation request"] --> B{"Who is assigning?"} B -->|Department lead| C["Lead can assign only to self or own department staff"] B -->|HCS or MD| D["Global assignment authority"] C --> E{"Assignment type"} D --> E E -->|Individual| F["Single owner task"] E -->|Multiple| G["Group task with task assignments"] E -->|Department| H["Department task"] F --> I["Task auto-links to assignee goal"] G --> J["Assigned users work shared task"] H --> K["Task supports department delivery"] I --> L["Status and completion contribute to individual KPI"] J --> L K --> M["Department task delivery contributes to department KPI"]

5. Reference Generator

Outgoing correspondence creation, approvals, reference generation, and requester notification.

flowchart TD A["User opens reference generator"] --> B["Sender name auto-fills from profile"] B --> C["User enters subject, recipient, category, and due date"] C --> D["Correspondence record is created as draft"] D --> E["Department lead is notified and reviews"] E --> F{"Department lead decision"} F -->|Approve| G["MD reviews the correspondence"] F -->|Reject| X["Draft returned or rejected"] G --> H{"MD decision"} H -->|Approve| I["Reference number is generated and stored"] H -->|Reject| X I --> J["Approved correspondence is sent back to requester"]

6. Individual KPI

Approved goals receive evidence automatically from assigned work.

flowchart TD A["Approved employee goals exist"] --> B["Work is created for employee"] B --> C["Manual task"] B --> D["Project task"] B --> E["Help desk synced task"] C --> F["System auto-links work to matching approved goal"] D --> F E --> F F --> G["Employee completes linked work"] G --> H["Each goal score is calculated from linked task completion"] H --> I["Individual KPI is the weighted average of approved goal scores"]

7. Individual PMS

Final employee performance score built from KPI, KSS, attendance, and behaviour.

flowchart TD A["Individual KPI"] --> E["Final PMS calculation"] B["KSS score from kss_results"] --> E C["Attendance score"] --> E D["Behaviour score from review"] --> E E --> F["If CBT is zero its weight is redistributed"] F --> G["Final individual PMS score is produced"] G --> H["Performance review is created"] H --> I["Employee acknowledges review"]

8. My Work

Unified personal work surface for executable assigned work.

flowchart TD A["User opens work page"] --> B["System loads assigned executable work"] B --> C["Manual tasks"] B --> D["Project tasks"] B --> E["Help desk synced tasks"] B --> F["Extra multi-assigned tasks"] C --> G["Items merge into My Work list"] D --> G E --> G F --> G G --> H["User filters by search, status, source, and priority"] H --> I{"User opens item"} I -->|Task| J["Navigate to task management"] I -->|Project task| K["Navigate to project detail"] I -->|Help desk| L["Navigate to help desk ticket detail"]

9. Goal

Goal moves from target-setting into an active KPI bucket with linked work evidence.

flowchart TD A["Employee or authorized manager creates goal"] --> B["Goal awaits approval"] B --> C["Lead or admin approves goal"] C --> D["Goal becomes active KPI target"] D --> E["System routes linked work into the goal"] E --> F["Manual tasks"] E --> G["Project tasks"] E --> H["Help desk synced tasks"] F --> I["Goal progress updates from task completion"] G --> I H --> I I --> J["Goal score contributes to individual KPI"]

10. Department PMS

Department performance score built from department KPI and supporting management factors.

flowchart TD A["Average individual KPI in department"] --> E["Department KPI"] B["Department action item score"] --> E C["Department help desk score"] --> E D["Department task and project delivery score"] --> E E --> F["Department PMS calculation"] G["Learning and capability score"] --> F H["Attendance and compliance score"] --> F I["Behaviour and leadership score"] --> F F --> J["Final department PMS score is produced"]
