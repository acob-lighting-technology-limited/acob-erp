Capacity Building Opportunity: Africa Climate Academy

Dear Team,

As part of HR/Admin’s commitment to staff capacity development, we would like to inform interested employees about an upcoming training opportunity.

Applications are currently open for the Africa Climate Academy, organized by the Africa Centre for Energy Policy.

Eligibility

  • Must have at least three years of relevant work experience
  • Must demonstrate interest in climate and sustainable energy
  • Must be available for the full one-week programme

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • CV/Resume
